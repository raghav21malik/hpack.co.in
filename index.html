About Us

Building Trust Through Quality Since 2006

A legacy of innovation, precision, and unwavering commitment to pharmaceutical packaging excellence.

Himalayan Packaging Industries Pvt. Ltd., the flagship company of the Himalayan Group of Industries, has been at the forefront of rigid plastic packaging manufacturing since 2006. Headquartered in New Delhi with state-of-the-art manufacturing plants in Selaqui (Dehradun) and Mandideep (Madhya Pradesh), we produce over 1.5 million containers every day.

Our expertise spans PET, PP, PC, HDPE, and LDPE containers serving the pharmaceutical, FMCG, cosmetic, food & beverage, liquor, and sports industries. With approximately 10% of India's pharmaceutical bottle market share and exports reaching Asia-Pacific, Africa, North America, Australia, and beyond, we are a trusted partner to leading brands worldwide.

We operate under stringent quality controls in our Class 10,000 Clean Room facility, using world-class Nissei ASB (Japan) Injection Stretch Blow Molding technology, ensuring every container meets the highest pharmaceutical standards.
